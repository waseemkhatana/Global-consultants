A Central Asian country, Tajikistan offers MBBS degree for students hailing from the other countries too. The lower fee structure, affordable cost of living, English mediated course, approval by Medical Council of India (MCI), World Health Organization (WHO) and United Nations Organization for Educational, Social and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) and Foundation for Advancement of International Medical Education and Research (FAIMER), with the state of art facilities and no donations are the main reasons attracting students from various parts of the world to study the MBBS degree in Tajikistan. The college also provides training for the screening test conducted by the Medical Council of India (MCI) for pursuing a career as a doctor in India. Also, the students are successful in clearing the USMLE examinations which will help the students to get placed in colleges in the United States of America for their further studies. The college in Tajikistan do not demand any qualification or passing of entrance examinations like the NEET, IELTS or TOEFL for admission to the college and is completely based on academic records. The students are trained by world class faculty that helps the students learn, absorb and serve the people in a better manner..
